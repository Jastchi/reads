**Main success factors of protests:**
- Large, diverse, and sustained group of participants;
- Variety of methods, including marches;
- Non-violence and loyalty shifts among security forces.

In particular, "no government has withstood a challenge of 3.5% of their population (...) during a peak event".

**Correlation vs. causation: Instrumental variables**
- How to isolate the causal link between protest turnout and political outcomes, as from the obvious correlation?
- Rainfall as an instrumental variable: Any correlation between rainfall and political outcome is clearly due to the causal effect of rainfall on protest turnout.
- Instrumental variables affect the dependent variable (political outcome) only via the independent variable (protest turnout).
