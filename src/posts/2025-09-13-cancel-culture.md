**How?**
- Self-reinforcing mechanism of spreading views by triggering shitstorms
- Status as a Service (Eugene Wei): Outrage offers far better returns on social status than real life.

**Mechanisms**
- Outrage entrepreneurship - strike at non-obvious targets;
- Purity spirals - Targets are closest ideologically but slightly to your right;
- Ponzi screaming - If you don't attack, fear that you're the closest target.

These dynamics are similar to the French Terror or the Chinese Cultural Revolution (although with much lower stakes).

**Duration**
- Start due to early technological adoption;
- End due to general conservative shift, and to Twitter -> X.
